ABOUT MALCOLM TODD

Malcolm Todd is a rising artist known for his genre-blending sound, smooth vocals, and charismatic stage presence. Growing up surrounded by music, he developed an early passion for songwriting and performance, shaping a style that blends pop, R&B, and alternative influences with a modern, youthful energy.

His music carries an intimate, storytelling feel, giving listeners a window into his thoughts, relationships, and the experiences that shaped him. Tracks like “Art Therapy,” “Roommates,” and “Fell in Love With a Girl” built his rapidly growing fanbase, resonating across social media and streaming platforms.

Today, Malcolm continues expanding his reach through new music, live performances, and a growing global community of listeners. Whether on stage or in the studio, he brings a sincerity and creative vision that define his sound and artistic identity.
